Places Around The School

the gym

The part of the school where Health and Physical Education(PE) are held. This is also the place where the concerts of Band, Chorus, and Orchestra are held.

the cafeteria

This is where the students eat lunch. And if they come to school early that day, they go to study hall to finish homework or read until school starts.

the front

This is where parents can enter the school during school hours to check out their children or to drop something off.
